Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam

The Missile Man of India

about

Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist
who served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007.
He was born on October 15, 1931, in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, India.

Achievements

  • Developed India's first satellite launch vehicle, SLV-3
  • Played a key role in the development of India's nuclear programme
  • Awarded the Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian honour
  • Served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007
